3D Printing & Design

Stencils, mechanical parts, organizational tools, and creative sculpts. I use 3D printing as both a practical tool and a creative outlet.

Zeus Goose

One of my favorite prints — a muscular goose (Zeus Goose) printed in PLA. This is the kind of project that starts as a joke and ends up being a genuinely fun modeling challenge.

Drawing Desk

When I got back into charcoal drawing, I needed a proper angled drawing surface. Rather than buy one, I designed and printed the hardware to convert my piano stand into a drawing desk. You can see it in action in the drawing section.
