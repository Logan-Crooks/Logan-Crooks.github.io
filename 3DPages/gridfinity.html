Gridfinity Bookshelf

This project is a modular Gridfinity-based bookshelf and storage system designed to organize tools, art supplies, hardware, consumables, medical supplies, games, and other small items. The goal of this system is to make everyday objects easier to find, access, and rearrange while keeping the storage layout flexible and expandable.

Each section below contains a different category of Gridfinity bins, organizers, and accessories. The system is designed so that bins can be moved, replaced, or expanded as the collection changes over time.

Project Categories

Tools

Storage bins and organizers for small tools, drivers, cutters, measuring tools, and repair supplies.

Art

Organizers for pencils, pens, brushes, erasers, markers, and other art materials.

Hardware

Bins for screws, nuts, bolts, washers, fasteners, and other small hardware components.

Consumables

Storage for commonly used materials such as tape, glue, blades, zip ties, and replacement parts.

Medical

Organized storage for basic medical supplies, first-aid items, and personal care materials.

Games

Gridfinity storage for dice, tokens, cards, miniatures, and tabletop gaming accessories.

Miscellaneous

General-purpose bins and organizers for items that do not fit cleanly into the other categories.
